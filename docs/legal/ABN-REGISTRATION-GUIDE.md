# How to Get an ABN and Register the Business Name "Woahh"
> Written for: Pawit Singh — Founding Member, Woahh
> Last updated: 2026-05-14

---

## Overview

An ABN (Australian Business Number) is registered in **your legal name** as a Sole Trader. You then register the trading name "Woahh" separately with ASIC. Once both are done, you are legally operating as:

> **Pawit Singh trading as Woahh** — ABN: [your 11-digit ABN]

This lets you:
- Purchase Supabase, Lovable, Resend, Clicksend, and other subscriptions under the Woahh business name
- Issue tax invoices to future customers
- Open a business bank account in the name "Woahh"
- Register `woahh.com.au` (requires an ABN matching the business name)
- Apply for Stripe as a business rather than an individual

**Total cost:** ~$42 (business name registration only — ABN itself is free)
**Total time:** ~30 minutes to complete both applications

---

## Part 1 — Apply for Your ABN

**URL:** https://abr.business.gov.au

### Step 1 — Open the ABN Application

1. Go to **abr.business.gov.au**
2. Click **"Apply for an ABN"** (top of the page or in the main navigation)
3. You will be redirected to the Australian Business Register application portal
4. Click **"Apply for an ABN"** on the next screen

You do not need a myGovID or login to apply — you fill in the form as a guest.

---

### Step 2 — Confirm Your Identity

You will be asked to confirm who is applying. Select:

> **Individual / Sole Trader**

Click **Next**.

---

### Step 3 — Enter Your Personal Details

Fill in exactly as follows:

| Field | Your Answer |
|---|---|
| Given name(s) | `Pawit` |
| Family name | `Singh` |
| Date of birth | [your date of birth] |
| Tax File Number (TFN) | [your personal TFN] |

> **Your TFN:** You will find this on your ATO correspondence, previous tax returns, or your myGov account under ATO. It is a 9-digit number. Providing your TFN is optional but **strongly recommended** — without it, the ATO will withhold tax at the highest rate from any business income.

Click **Next**.

---

### Step 4 — Your Business Address

| Field | Your Answer |
|---|---|
| Street address | `19 Sigwell Street` |
| Suburb | `Yarrabilba` |
| State | `QLD` |
| Postcode | `4207` |

This is your registered business address. You can update it later if you move.

Click **Next**.

---

### Step 5 — Business Activity

You will be asked to describe your main business activity. Use:

**Primary business activity (plain English description):**
> `Software as a service platform for hospitality and retail businesses`

You will also be asked to select an **ANZSIC code** (industry classification). Search for:
> `7372` — Software Publishing

Or alternatively:
> `7371` — Computer Programming Services

Either is fine for a SaaS platform. Select the closest match from the dropdown.

Click **Next**.

---

### Step 6 — Business Structure

When asked about your business structure, select:

> **Sole Trader**

This means the ABN is registered in your personal name. You are the sole owner and operator. This is the correct structure for now — before you incorporate as a Pty Ltd.

Click **Next**.

---

### Step 7 — GST Registration

You will be asked: *"Do you want to register for GST?"*

**Select: Yes — register for GST.**

> **Why:** At 100 merchants on the Marketplace plan ($89/month) you are at $8,900/month = $106,800/year — well above the $75,000 threshold where GST registration becomes mandatory. Register now to stay clean and to claim GST credits on your business expenses (Supabase, Lovable, Resend, hardware, etc.).

When asked about your GST reporting period:
> Select **Quarterly** — standard for a new small business

Your GST start date:
> Enter today's date

Click **Next**.

---

### Step 8 — Other Registrations (PAYG, FBT, LCT)

You will be asked about other ATO registrations:

| Registration | Select |
|---|---|
| PAYG Withholding (tax on employee wages) | **No** — you have no employees yet |
| Fringe Benefits Tax | **No** |
| Luxury Car Tax | **No** |
| Fuel Tax Credits | **No** |

You will register for PAYG Withholding when you hire your first employee.

Click **Next**.

---

### Step 9 — Review and Submit

A summary page will show all your details. Check:

- [ ] Your full legal name is correct: **Pawit Singh**
- [ ] Address is correct: **19 Sigwell Street Yarrabilba QLD 4207**
- [ ] Business structure: **Sole Trader**
- [ ] Business activity: matches what you entered
- [ ] GST: **Yes, registered from today**

Click **Submit**.

---

### Step 10 — Receive Your ABN

In most cases your ABN is **issued immediately** on screen after submission.

- Write it down and save it somewhere safe
- You will also receive a confirmation email from the ATO
- Your ABN will appear on the public **ABN Lookup** tool at abn.business.gov.au within 24 hours

**If your application is referred for review:** This happens if there is any question about your identity or the business activity. The ATO will contact you by email or phone within 5 business days. This is uncommon for a straightforward sole trader application.

## Part 2 — Register the Business Name "Woahh"

**Do this after your ABN is confirmed** — you need the ABN number to register a business name.

**URL:** https://connectonline.asic.gov.au

---

### Step 1 — Create an ASIC Connect Account

1. Go to **connectonline.asic.gov.au**
2. Click **"Register"** to create an account if you don't have one
3. You will need to verify your identity using a current driver's licence or passport
4. Once registered, log in

---

### Step 2 — Search for the Name First

Before applying, confirm "Woahh" is available:

1. From the ASIC Connect dashboard, click **"Search"**
2. Select **"Business Names"**
3. Search: `Woahh`
4. If it shows as available (no existing registrations), proceed
5. If it is taken, consider: `Woahh Australia`, `Woahh Platform`, or a slight variation — then continue

---

### Step 3 — Start the Business Name Application

1. From the ASIC Connect dashboard, click **"Start a new form"**
2. Select **"Register a business name"** (form type: **BN1**)

---

### Step 4 — Enter the Business Name

| Field | Your Answer |
|---|---|
| Business name to register | `Woahh` |

Click **Next**.

---

### Step 5 — Who Will Hold the Business Name

You will be asked who the business name holder is. Select:

> **Individual**

Enter your personal details:

| Field | Your Answer |
|---|---|
| Given name | `Pawit` |
| Family name | `Singh` |
| Date of birth | [your date of birth] |

---

### Step 6 — Link Your ABN

You will be asked to enter your ABN:

> Enter the 11-digit ABN you received in Part 1

ASIC will verify this automatically against the ABR. The ABN and business name will be linked in the public register.

---

### Step 7 — Business Address

Enter:

| Field | Your Answer |
|---|---|
| Street address | `19 Sigwell Street` |
| Suburb | `Yarrabilba` |
| State | `QLD` |
| Postcode | `4207` |

---

### Step 8 — Registration Period

You will be asked whether to register for 1 year or 3 years:

| Option | Cost | Recommendation |
|---|---|---|
| 1 year | ~$42 | Only if you plan to incorporate as Pty Ltd very soon (the Pty Ltd will hold the business name after that) |
| 3 years | ~$98 | **Recommended** — one less renewal to track; cheaper per year |

Select **3 years** unless you plan to incorporate within the next few months.

---

### Step 9 — Pay and Submit

Payment is by credit or debit card. The fee is charged immediately.

After payment, you will receive:
- An ASIC reference number
- Confirmation that "Woahh" is registered to you
- Your business name registration certificate (downloadable PDF)

The business name will appear on the ASIC register within 1–2 business days.

## Part 3 — After Both Are Done

Once you have your ABN and business name confirmed, do these immediately:

### Update Your ATO Profile with the Business Name

1. Log in to **myGov** → **ATO**
2. Go to **My profile → Business details**
3. Add your business name: `Woahh`
4. This links "Woahh" to your ABN on the public ABR lookup

Anyone searching your ABN will now see: **Pawit Singh trading as Woahh**.

---

### Save These Two Documents

You will need them repeatedly:

| Document | Where to get it |
|---|---|
| ABN confirmation letter | ATO email or myGov → ATO |
| Business name registration certificate | ASIC Connect dashboard → Your documents |

Keep PDF copies in `docs/legal/` in this repository.
